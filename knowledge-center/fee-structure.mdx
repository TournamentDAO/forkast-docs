## 💸 Fee Structure Overview

Forkast offers a clear and transparent fee structure that balances incentivizing active trading with maintaining the platform’s long-term sustainability.

**Note:** There are no fees currently as Forkast is running a zero fee promotional period through the end of July 2026. Outside of promotion periods, our standard fee structure is as follows:

| Fee                | Rate  |
| ------------------ | ----- |
| 💱 Trading Fee     | 0.50% |
| 🏧 Withdrawal Fee  | 0.25% |

### 💸 1. Trading Fee

- **Trading Fee**: A 0.50% fee applies when your sell order matches an existing order in the order book.
- The fee is charged when an order removes liquidity from the market by matching with an existing order.

**Note: Forkast covers all maker fees, meaning placing buy orders does not have a fee.**

### 💸 2. Withdrawal Fee

- A withdrawal fee of 0.25% is applied when converting Trading Credits (TC) to USDC, prior to withdrawing funds from the platform.

## 💡 Examples of Fees (if > 0)

A user places a sell order for 100 tokens priced at 1 TC each:

| Item             | Amount   |

| Item                    | Amount    |
| ----------------------- | --------- |

| Fee (0.25%)             | 0.25 TC   |
| **Net Redeemed**        | **99.75 USDC** |
