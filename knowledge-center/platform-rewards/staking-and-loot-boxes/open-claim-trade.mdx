**Claiming Loot Box Rewards**

The Forkast staking system rewards users with Loot Boxes based on their staked amount. Each week, a new claim window opens, allowing users to collect their rewards. It is essential to claim rewards within the claim window, as any unclaimed rewards will expire and become inaccessible.

### 🏦 Weekly Claim Windows

- Loot Boxes are generated based on amount and duration of staked batches.
- A new claim window starts every week.
- Users must open and claim their Loot Boxes before the next claim window begins.
- Expired rewards cannot be recovered.



![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/D-LdIq4FKOtov2npj4Ehr_image.png)

### 🎁 Open & Claim Loot Boxes - Two-Step Process

Loot Box claiming follows a two-step process:

1. **Open Loot Boxes**: Users initiate the opening process, revealing their potential rewards.
2. **Claim Rewards**: After opening, the claim button will appear on the rewards reveal modal. Users must complete this step to transfer rewards to their wallet.

Since these actions are executed onchain, transactions may take a few seconds. A loading spinner will appear within the button during processing. However, users are free to browse the platform and perform other actions while waiting for the transactions to complete—they are not locked into the process.



![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/JxdNgsbESbJnjvrh2A9EC_image.png)

### 🏦 Claim At Once

- Users may have multiple Loot Boxes for each type (Gold, Silver, Bronze) depending on their staking amount and duration.
- The open and claim process works as a bulk claim, meaning users do not need to open and claim each Loot Box individually.
- Once successfully claimed, the total rewards earned can be seen under total earnings section.

### 🎁 Receiving and Using Rewards

- After successful claiming, rewards will be available in the user's **Account Wallet**.
- Users can visit the **Deposit Page > Transfer Tab** to move their Credits from their Wallet to the **Forkast Trading Wallet**.
- Once transferred, users can start using their rewards on Forkast.



![You need to transfer your assets from Waypoint to Trading wallet to use them.](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/pDp19nM46QNsy5WFEPesQ_image.png "Transfer Rewards")

### 📌 Key Takeaways

- Claiming requires both **opening** and **claiming** the Loot Boxes.
- The claim window resets every week; unclaimed rewards expire.
- Transactions are onchain and may take a few seconds to process.
- Bulk claim ensures efficiency for users with multiple Loot Boxes.
- Rewards are deposited into the user's Account Wallet and can be transferred to the Forkast Trading Wallet for use.
