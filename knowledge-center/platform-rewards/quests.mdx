Forkast offers **Questing Rewards** to incentivize users for completing specific challenges and milestones on the platform. Quests are designed to engage users, encourage consistent participation, and reward skillful trading. **Quest rewards are paid out in USDC or Trading Credits.**

Also on the quest page, you can also check your Referral status, Credits earned from referees, and claim your Leadearboard rewards. 

***

### 🗺️ Available Quests

1. **Link Your Twitter Account**
   - Objective: Connect your Twitter account to your Forkast profile. 
   - Frequency: One-time quest.
2. **Place in the Top 50 on the Weekly Leaderboard**
   - Objective: Rank among the top 50 users on the weekly leaderboard.
   - Frequency: Weekly.
3. **Place in the Top 50 on the Monthly Leaderboard**
   - Objective: Rank among the top 50 users on the monthly leaderboard.
   - Frequency: Monthly.
4. **Win in X Markets**
   - Objective: Successfully predict the outcome of X markets during the quest period.
   - Frequency: Weekly or monthly, depending on the quest.
5. **Place X Trades**
   - Objective: Execute X trades on the platform during the quest period. Must be across diferrent markets. 
   - Frequency: Weekly or monthly, depending on the quest.
6. **Make a Profit on X Trades**
   - Objective: Achieve a profit on X trades during the quest period.
   - Frequency: Weekly or monthly, depending on the quest.

***

Forkast Questing Rewards system offers diverse challenges that cater to all types of traders, ensuring an engaging and rewarding experience for everyone.
