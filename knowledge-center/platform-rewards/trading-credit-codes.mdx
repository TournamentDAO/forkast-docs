### 🪙 Trading Credit Codes

**Trading Credit Codes** are designed to reward users and foster engagement through collaborations with content creators, partners, and community campaigns. You can redeem a Platform Credit Code on the deposit page.

![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/UBiUwZmH65nkFVo6josKS_image.png)

***

### 🧭 Overview

- **Unique Codes**: Each Trading Credit Code is unique and generated by the Forkast platform.
- **Distribution**: Codes can be distributed to users, influencers, partners, or through promotional campaigns.
- **Redemption**: Codes allow users to claim **Trading Credits (TC)**, which can be used for trading on the platform.

***

### ⭐ Key Features

1. **Customizable Redemption**
 - Some codes are **single-use**, meaning they can only be redeemed by one user.
 - Others are **multi-use**, allowing multiple users to redeem the same code.
2. **Ease of Use**
 - Users can redeem codes directly on the platform via the **Redeem Code** section.
3. **Flexible Distribution**
   - Codes can be issued for various purposes, such as:
 - **User Rewards**: To incentivize participation in events or campaigns.
 - **Promotional Campaigns**: Distributed by partners or KOLs to engage their audiences.
 - **Partnership Activations**: Provided to partners for onboarding or collaboration incentives.
