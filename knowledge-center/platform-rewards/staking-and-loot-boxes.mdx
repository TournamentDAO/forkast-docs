## 👋 Introduction

Forkast's staking system enables users to stake to earn weekly rewards inside of Loot Boxes. Higher and longer staked amounts unlock better rewards. Loot Boxes are categorized as Bronze, Silver, or Gold, each requiring different minimum staked amounts to unlock.

Loot Box distributions occur weekly and may be opened at no cost, with rewards automatically deposited into the user's account wallet. Read more about how to use your rewards for trading here: [Open, Claim -> Trade!](/knowledge-center/platform-rewards/staking-and-loot-boxes/open-claim-trade) 

***

## 🪙 Staking

Users can stake for a predetermined period of time. The longer the staking period, the higher the reward multiplier:

- 1 Month = 1x
- 3 Months = 1.5x
- 6 Months = 2x

The longer you stake, the better the Loot Boxes you can access, and the higher your potential rewards.

***

## 🎁 Loot Boxes

Rewards are distributed across three different types of Loot Boxes: Bronze, Silver, and Gold, each offering increasingly valuable rewards.

- **Bronze Loot Box**
- **Silver Loot Box**
- **Gold Loot Box**

The staking amount and locking period together determine the type and tier of Loot Boxes user earn. Reward multipliers are applied based on the staking period.

**Disclaimer: The Loot Boxes awarded during Forkast's launch were unique and one-of-a-kind.**

## 🎁 Benefits of Staking & Loot Box Rewards

- **Exciting Incentives:** Gamified rewards make staking more exciting.
- **Scalable and Secure:** Decentralized infrastructure ensures fairness and transparency.
- **High ROI:** Longer staking periods and higher amounts yield greater rewards.

## 🎁 Loot Box Rewards Claiming

Loot Boxes are awarded weekly. Their reward preview will appear in the bottom-right section of the Staking page, together with summarized historical Loot Box earnings.

Claiming a Loot Box involves two simple steps: opening and claiming. Opening reveals the drop rates, while claiming transfers the rewards directly to your wallet. Both steps are completely free and gasless.

Once the claim period begins, an "Open Loot Box" button will appear, guiding you through the process. Opening the Loot Box will display your actual rewards. Once you hit the "Claim Rewards" button, these rewards will be sent to your account wallet.
