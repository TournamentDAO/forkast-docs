title: "👤 Profile Page"
description: >-
  Explore the Profile Page to view your wallet details, portfolio value, cash
  balance, profit/loss, trading volume, and position history.
---

The Profile Page serves as a central hub for user information, consolidating all details regarding their trading activities and current positions.

![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/onxYp-WkvAf6bi81AXdmX_image.png)

Specifically, the profile page shows the following information about the user:

- 👛 **Wallet Address** — Your unique wallet identifier, displayed prominently for easy reference.
- ✏️ **Edit Profile** — Customize your personal details or update your preferences.
- 📊 **Portfolio Overview** — A snapshot of your positions and assets at a glance.
- 💼 **Total Portfolio Value** — The combined value of your holdings and investments (e.g., $13,397).
- 💵 **Cash Balance** — Trading Credits available for use (e.g., $13,249).
- 🔄 **Deposit/Withdraw Funds** — Quick access to the Deposit page.
- 📈 **Profit/Loss** — A snapshot of your net trading profit or loss (e.g., +$11.8).
- 📉 **Trading Volume** — The total value of trades executed (e.g., $136).
- 🎯 **Predictions Traded** — A count of all trades you've placed so far on the platform (e.g., 3).
- 🏆 **Gains** — How your trades have performed over time (e.g., +8.66%).

Furthermore, the lower half of the page shows more details about your activity and portfolio breakdown:

- 📌 **Current Positions** — Positions held across all markets.
- 🕘 **Historical Activity** — Your trading, rewards, and other activity on the platform.
