## 📊 What is a Central Limit Order Book (CLOB)?

A Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) is an advanced trading infrastructure that maintains a centralized ledger of all buy and sell orders for a specific market, organized by price and time priority. On Forkast, we utilize a specialized variant called the Binary Limit Order Book (BLOB), which is specifically optimized for prediction market trading with binary outcomes.

Unlike traditional trading platforms, Forkast's CLOB operates through a hybrid-decentralized model where order matching occurs offchain for optimal speed and efficiency, while settlement happens onchain to ensure transparency and security. This architecture provides users with the precision of traditional financial markets combined with the trustless nature of blockchain technology.

## 🔮 Prediction Markets and Events

At the heart of Forkast are **prediction markets**, where users trade shares based on binary outcomes (e.g., "YES" or "NO"). These markets are organized into **Events**, which aggregate multiple markets under a single theme, such as the winner of a sports match. This structure enables users to explore and trade on various aspects of the same topic.

## 🔄 Trading and Order Lifecycle

Forkast.gg supports both **market orders** (instant execution at current prices) and **limit orders** (executed only when a specified price is met). All orders are processed through a centralized **order book**, matched off-chain for efficiency, and settled onchain for transparency. The **order lifecycle** ensures fairness and reliability, allowing users to manage trades effectively.

## 👛 Proxy Wallets and Conditional Tokens

To enhance the user experience, Forkast employs **proxy wallets**. These smart contract wallets handle complex transactions like splitting collateral into binary **conditional tokens**, merging tokens back into collateral, and redeeming payouts. Proxy wallets simplify multi-step processes while ensuring users maintain full control over their assets.
