## 🔮 What is Forkast?

[Forkast](https://forkast.gg/) is a prediction market that lets you trade on the outcomes of future events, spanning live esports, gaming, finance, and crypto trends.

Prediction markets use the collective intelligence of participants to create accurate probability estimates about the likelihood of different outcomes. Unlike gambling, which relies predominantly on chance, prediction markets reward those with deep domain knowledge and superior analytical skills.

With Forkast, you can trade in a secure and transparent environment on topics you love. Events feature outcomes that range from simple yes/no questions to multiple-choice options. To get started, simply select a market and place a trade on the outcome you believe will occur. When the market resolves, you’ll receive $1.00 for every share you hold, provided your prediction is correct.

**How are probabilities determined in a market?**
In a market, probabilities are shaped by the collective actions of traders. As people buy and sell, the market adjusts to reflect the perceived likelihood of each outcome. For instance, if more users purchase "Yes" shares, the market assigns a higher probability to "Yes" being the result. Conversely, increased demand for "No" shares will raise the likelihood of "No" as the outcome. These probabilities are dynamic, constantly shifting as new trades and information flow in, offering a real-time snapshot of the collective expectations about what is most likely to occur.

## ⛓️ Blockchain

**Which blockchain is Forkast built on?**

Forkast is built on [Arbitrum](https://arbitrum.io/), a high-speed, low-cost blockchain network built to scale Ethereum. Arbitrum gives Forkast the performance needed for consumer trading while still benefiting from Ethereum’s broader security and ecosystem.

For users, that means faster transactions, lower fees, and a smoother experience compared with trading directly on Ethereum mainnet. This makes Arbitrum a strong fit for prediction markets, where speed, cost, and reliability matter every time a user enters or exits a position.

**What is USDC, and where can I buy it?**
USDC is a digital dollar (stablecoin) that's always worth $1 USD (1 USDC = $1 USD). 

You can buy USDC from crypto exchanges or payment processors such as the ones found on the deposit page at forkast.gg/deposit. Once you have USDC, deposit it into Forkast and use your Trading Credits to trade. Winnings can be withdrawn back to USDC when available.

**FAQ: I redeemed 500 TC, but only 499.99 showed up in my wallet – why does that happen?**

Due to the nature of blockchain transactions, **TC (Trading Credits) supports up to 6 decimal places**, and trading fees are also calculated with decimal precision. This can sometimes lead to **small rounding differences** between the amount displayed on Forkast and the actual on-chain amount.

For example:

- You might see a **position quantity of 500 TC** on Forkast, but the on-chain balance may be **499.999999 TC** due to fractional fee calculations.
- This **does not affect the actual value of your holdings**—the blockchain maintains accuracy, even if the displayed number rounds slightly.

This behavior is common across other blockchain platforms, and we are actively exploring ways to minimize the impact. If you have any concerns, feel free to reach out to our support team. To learn more visit [Sign Up on Forkast](/knowledge-center/sign-up-on-forkast).

**Why am I seeing the error message "You have reached the maximum amount allowed" when trying to deposit more USDC into my Forkast account?**

This means your account has reached its maximum platform credit purchase limit. Once your total deposits hit this cap, you’ll encounter this error. If you believe you haven’t yet reached the limit but are still seeing this error, please contact our support team for assistance.

**What is the difference between a Market Order and a Limit Order?**
**Market Orders**
A market order is used to buy or sell shares immediately at the best available price.
*Example:* If you place a market order to buy 60 shares of "SKT T1-Win, Yes," the system will instantly match your order with the best offer in the order book.


**Limit Orders**
A limit order allows you to set a specific price to buy or sell shares. The trade will only happen if someone matches your price.
*Example:* If you place a limit order to buy 75 shares of "SKT T1-Win, Yes" at $0.50, the order will remain open until a seller is willing to sell at that price.

**How does the Forkast referral program work?**
To earn referral rewards, the person you refer (Person B) must meet specific criteria. Person B can either spend a minimum of $5 USDC on the platform, such as through trading, converting USDC to Trading Credits, or participating in markets. Once these requirements are fulfilled, you (Person A) become eligible for the referral rewards. 

**What do I win by referring others?**
When your referral (Person B) meets the criteria to become a successful referral, both of you receive rewards. As the referrer (Person A), you earn 10% of the trading fees generated by Person B for the next 6 months. These rewards are paid in Trading Credits, which can be used for trading on Forkast.

Meanwhile, your referral (Person B) gets a 25% discount on trading fees for the first 3 months after signing up. It’s a win-win for both parties!

To learn more visit [Referral System: How It Works](/knowledge-center/platform-rewards/referral-system-how-it-works)

## 💼 Portfolio & Shares

**What do I need to do before I can start trading?**
To start trading, create an account using your email / social login / wallets . After that, deposit USDC into your Forkast account to begin trading. 

**I've changed my mind about a trade. Can I sell my shares?**
Yes, you can sell your shares anytime before the market resolves at the best available price.

**When does a market close?**
Markets close at the time specified on the market page; users can verify this time on the page itself or once the outcome is officially confirmed by a reputable source.

**What are my shares worth if I hold them until the market is resolved?**
Winning shares always resolve to $1.00 each. For example, shares purchased at $0.52 will be worth $1.00 each if the outcome is correct, or $0 if the outcome is incorrect when the market resolves.

**Can I own both ‘Yes’ and ‘No’ shares for a market at the same time?**
Yes, holding both positions is possible.

**I received an error when I submitted my trade. What do I do?**
Retry the transaction after a few seconds. If the issue persists, contact Forkast support.

**Where can I check my activity and order history?**
You can track your open buy and sell orders and view your market participation on the **Portfolio** page. Inside, you'll find tabs like **"Open Orders"** and **"Activity"** to navigate your transaction history.

Additionally, you can share your positions directly on social media using the **"Share"** button.

## ⚖️ Resolution

**What happens when a market resolves?**
When resolved, winning shares pay out $1.00 each. Losing shares are worth $0.

**What should I do when a market resolves?**
Claim your winnings by redeeming your shares from your Portfolio page.

**What happens to unfulfilled buy orders when a market resolves?**
If a market resolves before your buy order is fulfilled, the order is automatically canceled, and your funds are refunded.
