## Step 1: Choose Sign Up Option

**How do I create an account?**
Head over to [Forkast](https://forkast.gg/). Click the Sign Up button at the top right. You can sign up using an email-based login, Google SSO, or Metamask Wallet. 

Keep in mind that the method you select will need to be used for all future sign-ins, so pick the option that works best for you. Email-based login is recommended for new users.

![](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/XoS_d3cxHuX3nLYcnVQQy_image.png)

***Email Address Signup*** : Prefer email? Just enter your address and confirm the one-time password. No extensions, no wallets, no seed phrases. Quick and easy.

[Metamask Wallet Sign Up:](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ronin-wallet/fnjhmkhhmkbjkkabndcnnogagogbneec?hl=en) Sign up by linking your already installed [Ronin or Metamask Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ronin-wallet/fnjhmkhhmkbjkkabndcnnogagogbneec) extension to Forkast. Both of these wallets are self-managed wallets available as a Chrome browser extension. If you choose to use this sign up option, be sure to back up your private key to securely maintain ownership of your assets.

***Google Signup*** : Sign up instantly with your Google account—no browser extensions, wallet setup, or seed phrases required.

## Step 2: Link Wallet & Create Account

Once you’ve linked your wallet or created your account using Google SSO or email signup in Step 1, continue by choosing a username and entering your email address (required). To proceed, review our privacy policy and check the box to provide your consent. You may also choose to opt in to marketing emails. When you're ready, click **“Continue” **&#x74;o move to the final step of account creation.



![](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/80rnxvi8sVNl_o9IhOB-8_screenshot-2025-12-01-143414.png)

## Step 3: Create Trading Wallet

Before accessing the platform, you’ll need to create your Trading Wallet, also known as a proxy wallet. This wallet will serve as your primary account for trading using our platform currency, Platform Credits. Simply complete this quick, one-time setup to activate your wallet and start trading!.

![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/ikV2hDBQf6j1yxivNctOp-UWq68rX1QIjUQCCqUWNBc-20250705-211040.jpg)

**What is a proxy account and why do I need it?**
A proxy account acts as a middleman between your wallet and the Forkast platform. It helps ensure secure interactions, making the platform more efficient and easier to use while maintaining decentralization. 

# Final Step: 

## Approve Platform Credits and Start Trading!

- The Final step is a one-time Token Approval to enable trading of Trading Credits in your Trading Wallet. This simple step allows Forkast to securely access your Trading Credits, ensuring seamless and secure transactions moving forward.
- Trading Credits are backed 1:1 with USDC and are used for trading in markets.

## If You're Having Trouble with Blocked Pop Ups

The Forkast platform is designed with a seamless and user-friendly wallet experience that does not require users to install wallet browser extensions. Instead, it relies on an novel **non-extension wallet interface**. When engaging with the platform, the wallet interface automatically pops up as a **new tab** or a **new window**, allowing users to interact with the platform without needing any additional setup or downloads.

To ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience, it is essential to adjust your browser settings to allow **Forkast** to open additional popups. If popups are blocked, the wallet interface may not appear, interrupting the transaction flow and user experience.

### Steps to Ensure Proper Configuration:

1. **Check Popup Settings:** Navigate to your browser’s settings and ensure that popups are not blocked for forkast.gg.
   - For Chrome: Go to **Settings > Privacy and Security > Site Settings > Pop-ups and redirects**, and add **https\://www\.forkast.gg** as an allowed site.

![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/dc3FuxF6tjdr3iJi0YO5k_image.png)

- For Safari: Make sure you have the forkast.gg page open. Then, open **Settings > Websites > Pop-up Windows**, and set **https\://www\.forkast.gg** to "Allow."

![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/A5vB56NRK83TyJpiyShea_image.png)

- For Firefox: Go to **Settings > Privacy & Security > Permissions**, and ensure popups are enabled for **https\://www\.forkast.gg**.

![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/9iBxGyOic4cRFh1ToOVqV/r9gY7p-RpMQs_g_ZvbRPT_image.png)

2. **Whitelist Forkast:** If your browser uses a third-party ad blocker or privacy extension, ensure that **forkast.gg** is added to its whitelist to prevent interference.
3. **Enable Popups Temporarily:** Some browsers offer an option to enable popups when they are blocked. Be sure to click "Allow" when prompted during your first interaction with the platform.

By configuring your browser to support this functionality, you can enjoy the full benefits of the Forkast, including secure, efficient, and frictionless interactions with its wallet system.
